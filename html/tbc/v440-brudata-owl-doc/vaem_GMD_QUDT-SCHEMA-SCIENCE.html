dcterms:description
The science domain of QUDT defines the base classes properties, and restrictions used for modeling physical quantities, units of measure, and their dimensions for upper level domains of science and mathematics. The goal of the QUDT ontology is to provide a unified model of, measurable quantities, units for measuring different kinds of quantities, the numerical values of quantities in different units of measure and the data structures and data types used to store and manipulate these objects in software. Except for unit prefixes, all units are specified in separate vocabularies. Descriptions are provided in both HTML and LaTeX formats.
